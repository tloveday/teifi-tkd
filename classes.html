Tigers
Tuesday & Thursday 5:00:pm - 6:00pm 4 - 6

Our Tigers programme has been specifically created to be interactive and engaging for our youngest of Martial Artists.

The Tiger's learn through a variety of games and activities that introduce the techniques and skills which will set them up for our older classes.

The programme designed for the Tigers goes beyond the teaching of Martial Arts and self-defence to include the teaching of self-protection, important life skills and character education.

Every parent’s paramount concern is their children’s safety, so we include safety orientated lessons that focus on topics such as dealing with bullies and strangers.

Juniors
Tuesday & Thursday 6:15pm - 7:15pm 6 - 13

Our junior classes are perfect for children aged 6 – 13 years old. They will have fun and meet other children with similar interests, whilst burning away that abundance of energy before bed time.

Children who are new to taekwondo will be taught the basics like how to block, strike, punch and kick. Then they work through the syllabus with our instructors, they will be taught more advanced techniques and patterns to progress.

Every parent’s paramount concern is their children’s safety, so we include safety orientated lessons that focus on topics such as dealing with bullies and strangers.

Seniors
Tuesday & Thursday 7:30pm - 9:00pm 13+

Our Senior class encompasses everything found in our juniors class but with the adults instead. We teach all the basic, intermediate and advanced techniques in order to give you the skills to make you a rounded Martial Artist and on the road to eventually earning a Black Belt, which is only the first chapter on the journey.

Whether your a beginner, have limited experience or a seasoned veteran there is plenty of space on our mats.
